# Competitive Pricing Research: Fitness Gloves

## Initial Findings (Jan 2026)

### Major Competitors
*   **Gymshark:**
    *   Standard Lifting Gloves: ~$26 (often on sale for ~$10-18)
    *   Legacy Lifting Gloves: ~$22 - $44 (higher end)
    *   Positioning: Mass market, trendy, frequent sales.
*   **Lululemon:**
    *   Wunder Train Padded Gloves: $38
    *   Running/Training Gloves: $42 - $58
    *   Positioning: Premium, high brand value, "technical" fabrics.
*   **Nike:**
    *   Elemental Fitness Gloves: ~$20
    *   Positioning: Accessible, functional, ubiquitous.
*   **G-Loves:**
    *   Regular Price: ~$60
    *   Positioning: "Made with love in the USA", specialized for women, premium niche.
*   **Amazon / Mass Market (Harbinger, etc.):**
    *   Range: $12 - $28
    *   Positioning: Pure utility, budget-focused.

### Price Point Analysis for ANCHR Gear ($34.99)
*   **Comparison:**
    *   Significantly higher than generic Amazon/Walmart options ($12-15).
    *   Higher than standard Nike/Gymshark entry-level ($20-26).
    *   **Comparable to:** Lululemon's entry-level ($38) and Gymshark's "Legacy" line ($44).
    *   **Lower than:** Specialized boutique brands like G-Loves ($60).

### Initial Thoughts
*   $34.99 places ANCHR Gear in the **"Premium Essential"** category.
*   To justify this price, the gloves cannot look generic. They must compete with Lululemon's aesthetic and Gymshark's higher-tier "Legacy" line.
