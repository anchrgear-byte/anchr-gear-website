## THE PHILOSOPHY: NO GYM. NO EXCUSES.

You don't need a $100/month gym membership to change your life. You don't need a room full of chrome machines. You need gravity, intensity, and a plan.

This program is built for the "Grind." It's for the person who has 45 minutes before the kids wake up, or 45 minutes after a long shift. It's for the person who is rebuildingâ€”whether that's from weight loss, injury, or just years of neglect.

We focus on **Progressive Overload**. This means doing more over time. More reps, more sets, less rest, or better form. We use minimal tools: Dumbbells, ANCHR Battle Ropes, and your own Bodyweight.

### EQUIPMENT NEEDED
1.  **Dumbbells:** A pair of adjustable dumbbells or a few fixed pairs (light, medium, heavy).
2.  **ANCHR Battle Ropes:** For conditioning, power, and core stability.
3.  **Bench or Sturdy Chair:** For support during presses and rows.
4.  **Yoga Mat:** For floor work.

## THE WARM-UP (DO THIS BEFORE EVERY SESSION)
*Duration: 5-7 Minutes*

Never start cold. A cold engine breaks.

1.  **Jumping Jacks:** 60 seconds
2.  **Arm Circles:** 30 seconds forward, 30 seconds backward
3.  **Bodyweight Squats:** 15 reps (slow and controlled)
4.  **Walkouts (Inchworms):** 10 reps (stand tall, walk hands out to plank, walk back)
5.  **World's Greatest Stretch:** 5 reps per side

---

## PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (WEEKS 1-4)
**GOAL:** Stability, Form Correction, Tendon Strength.
**SCHEDULE:** 3 Days/Week (e.g., Mon, Wed, Fri).
**REST:** 60-90 seconds between sets.

In this phase, do not chase heavy weights. Chase perfect reps. If your form breaks, the set is over.

### WORKOUT A (MONDAY) - PUSH & LEGS
1.  **Goblet Squats:** 3 sets of 12-15 reps
    *   *Cue: Hold dumbbell at chest. Sit back like you're sitting in a chair.*
2.  **Push-Ups (Knees or Toes):** 3 sets of 10-12 reps
    *   *Cue: Chest to floor. Elbows tucked at 45 degrees.*
3.  **Dumbbell Overhead Press (Seated or Standing):** 3 sets of 12 reps
    *   *Cue: Core tight. Don't arch your back.*
4.  **Dumbbell Lunges:** 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
5.  **ANCHR Rope Waves (Alternating):** 3 sets of 30 seconds
    *   *Cue: Fast hands. Keep the waves moving all the way to the anchor point.*

### WORKOUT B (WEDNESDAY) - PULL & CORE
1.  **Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift (RDL):** 3 sets of 12-15 reps
    *   *Cue: Hips back. Feel the stretch in your hamstrings. Back flat.*
2.  **Single-Arm Dumbbell Row:** 3 sets of 12 reps per arm
    *   *Cue: Pull elbow to hip. Squeeze your back at the top.*
3.  **Glute Bridges:** 3 sets of 15 reps (hold for 2s at top)
4.  **Plank Hold:** 3 sets of 30-45 seconds
5.  **ANCHR Rope Slams:** 3 sets of 20 reps
    *   *Cue: Lift high, slam hard. Use your whole body.*

### WORKOUT C (FRIDAY) - FULL BODY CONDITIONING
1.  **Dumbbell Thrusters (Squat to Press):** 3 sets of 12 reps
2.  **Renegade Rows (or Plank Rows):** 3 sets of 10 reps per side
3.  **Mountain Climbers:** 3 sets of 45 seconds
4.  **ANCHR Rope Snakes (Floor Sweeps):** 3 sets of 30 seconds
5.  **Russian Twists (with or without weight):** 3 sets of 20 reps total

## PHASE 2: HYPERTROPHY (WEEKS 5-8)
**GOAL:** Muscle Growth, Volume, Metabolic Stress.
**SCHEDULE:** 4 Days/Week (e.g., Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri).
**REST:** 45-60 seconds between sets.

We are increasing the volume. The weights should feel heavier now. The last 2 reps of every set should be a struggle.

### WORKOUT A (MON/THU) - UPPER BODY FOCUS
1.  **Dumbbell Chest Press (Floor or Bench):** 4 sets of 10-12 reps
2.  **Bent Over Dumbbell Rows:** 4 sets of 10-12 reps
3.  **Lateral Raises:** 3 sets of 15 reps
    *   *Cue: Pour the pitcher. Lead with elbows.*
4.  **Bicep Curls:** 3 sets of 12 reps
5.  **Tricep Overhead Extensions:** 3 sets of 12 reps
6.  **ANCHR Rope Power Slams:** 4 sets of 30 seconds (All out effort)

### WORKOUT B (TUE/FRI) - LOWER BODY FOCUS
1.  **Dumbbell Sumo Squats:** 4 sets of 12 reps
    *   *Cue: Wide stance. Toes out. Target inner thighs and glutes.*
2.  **Stiff-Legged Deadlifts:** 4 sets of 12 reps
3.  **Bulgarian Split Squats (or Static Lunges):** 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
    *   *Cue: The "King" of leg exercises. Embrace the burn.*
4.  **Calf Raises:** 3 sets of 20 reps
5.  **ANCHR Rope Jumping Jacks:** 4 sets of 30 seconds
    *   *Cue: Hold rope ends, perform jumping jacks. Timing is key.*

---

## PHASE 3: POWER & SHRED (WEEKS 9-12)
**GOAL:** Fat Loss, Explosiveness, Definition.
**SCHEDULE:** 4 Days/Week (Upper/Lower Split) or 3 Days Full Body HIIT.
**REST:** 30-45 seconds. Keep moving.

This is the "Grind." We combine strength with high-intensity intervals.

### WORKOUT A (MON/THU) - EXPLOSIVE UPPER
1.  **Push-Up to Renegade Row:** 4 sets of 8 reps per side
2.  **Dumbbell Snatch (Single Arm):** 4 sets of 10 reps per arm
    *   *Cue: Use your hips to launch the weight up. Catch it high.*
3.  **Arnold Press:** 3 sets of 12 reps
4.  **ANCHR Rope Tabata:** 20 seconds WORK, 10 seconds REST. Repeat 8 times (4 minutes total).
    *   *Alternating Waves*

### WORKOUT B (TUE/FRI) - EXPLOSIVE LOWER
1.  **Jump Squats (Bodyweight):** 4 sets of 15 reps
2.  **Dumbbell Step-Ups (Chair or Bench):** 4 sets of 12 reps per leg
3.  **Kettlebell/Dumbbell Swings:** 4 sets of 20 reps
    *   *Cue: Hinge at the hips. Snap forward. Glutes tight.*
4.  **ANCHR Rope Burpee Slams:** 4 sets of 10 reps
    *   *Cue: Perform a slam, drop to burpee, pop up, slam again. This is the finisher.*

## COOL DOWN (DO THIS AFTER EVERY SESSION)
*Duration: 5 Minutes*

1.  **Child's Pose:** Hold for 60 seconds
2.  **Cat-Cow Stretch:** 10 slow cycles
3.  **Seated Hamstring Stretch:** 30 seconds per leg
4.  **Doorway Chest Stretch:** 30 seconds per side
5.  **Deep Breathing:** 1 minute (Inhale 4s, Hold 4s, Exhale 4s)
